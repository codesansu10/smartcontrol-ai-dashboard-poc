# SMARTCONTROL 2.0 AI Dashboard

Frontend PoC visual for AI-enabled process redesign for OEKOBIT / SMARTCONTROL 2.0.

## What The Dashboard Shows

The mockup presents a university-ready SaaS dashboard with static, realistic process data. It is designed to demonstrate how AI can support operational workflows without replacing expert validation or operator responsibility.

## Included Processes

- AI Anomaly Detection: AI detects unusual plant behavior, OEKOBIT experts validate alerts, and plant operators decide on operational action.
- AI-assisted Monthly Expert Reporting: AI drafts and structures reports, experts interpret and approve, then final reports are delivered to customers.
- Standard Customer Status Updates: Low-risk routine updates can be drafted and sent automatically, while uncertain or higher-risk content is routed to expert review.

## Oversight Model

- AI supports detection, drafting, and routine automation.
- Experts validate important AI outputs before escalation or delivery.
- Operators remain responsible for operational process decisions.
- Responsible AI safeguards are visible through data quality, human oversight, accountability, transparency, cybersecurity, GDPR, and adoption cards.
